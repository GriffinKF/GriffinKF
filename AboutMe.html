Early Life
I was born in the city of Islamabad in Pakistan, and although I chose to have a Pakistani flag represet the "Early Life" section of this about me page. That's about the only relation I really have with my home country. My parents moved to Qatar before I was even a year old, the background of this page is actually one of the Doha Skyline. Doha was where I lived for 19 years, where I went to school and did everything before coming to Dundee for University.
Studies
In high school and during my IGCSE (I for international) I had a pretty basic subject spread. Sciences/Maths/Humanities. Little bit of everything. I only really started taking an interest in computing as my school offered the ability to complete the course in one year and sit the exam early. Which was appealing to me, and from there, I guess the interest grew, here I am today, studying CS. Although, even though it may have been my first choice, it wasn't my dream subject
The Dream
I've always wanted to make movies. In fact, that is the dream subject I was talking about earlier, but being an international student and with how international fees go, neither myself nor my parents really saw studying film to be viable. However, I maintain a keen interest in the film making arts and read a lot about it in my spare time, one day when I have a study CS job, I might even try to make my own movie.
My Work
University is expensive, especially if you're an internatinal student. So, I've maintained a somewhat strange source of income. I work for a website known as Guardian.Services (that's what the orange skull logo is from), why is it strange you ask? Well, I get paid to play video games. In this case, a game known as Destiny. My go to explanation is that people are bad at video games, so they pay me to be good for them. The work I do is known as boosting or account recovering, and I take a small amount of pride in the fact that the general community hates what people like me do, it tickles my ego.
Hobbies

Other games include FIFA, which I play because I both enjoy watching and playing football in real life.
